# Getting Started as a VibeCoder

Welcome to VibeTalent — the marketplace where your projects and consistency speak louder than any resume.

## What is VibeTalent?

VibeTalent is a platform where developers (we call them **vibecoders**) build a public reputation based on what actually matters: **what you've shipped** and **how consistently you code**. Clients browse the platform to find reliable vibecoders they can trust.

Instead of writing a resume, you build a track record that proves itself.

## Step 1: Sign Up

Head to [vibetalent.work](https://vibetalent.work) and click **Create Profile**.

You can sign up with:

- **GitHub** (recommended) — we'll pull your avatar and username automatically
- **Google** — quick and easy

That's it. No lengthy onboarding forms.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

Once you're in, head to your **Dashboard** and fill out your profile:

| Field | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| **Username** | This is your public handle — it appears in your profile URL (`vibetalent.work/profile/you`) |
| **Bio** | A short description of who you are and what you build. Clients read this first. |
| **Avatar** | A profile photo makes you look real and trustworthy |
| **Social Links** | Connect your Twitter, GitHub, Telegram, website, and Farcaster so clients can reach you |

**Tip:** Fill out every field. Complete profiles rank higher and get more attention from clients.

## Step 3: Add Your First Project

Projects are the **primary driver of your Vibe Score** and the most important thing clients look at. Head to the **Projects** section of your Dashboard and add a project you've built.

For the best Vibe Score boost, include:

- A clear **title** and **description** (50+ characters)
- Your **tech stack** (at least 3 technologies)
- A **live URL** where people can see it in action
- A **GitHub URL** so people can see the code
- A **screenshot** so it looks great on your profile

**Pro tip:** Verified projects (where you prove GitHub ownership) earn **5x more points** than unverified ones. A single well-documented project is worth more than weeks of streak points.

## Step 4: Start Your Streak

On your Dashboard, you'll see a **Log Activity** button. Click it to record that you coded today.

This starts your **streak** — the number of consecutive days you've logged coding activity. Streaks contribute to your reputation and help you earn badges on VibeTalent.

You can also [connect your GitHub](github-sync.md) to log activity automatically whenever you push code, open PRs, or create repos.

## Step 5: Get Discovered

Once your profile is set up, you're automatically listed on:

- **Explore page** — where clients search and filter vibecoders by skills, badges, and Vibe Score
- **Leaderboard** — where the most prolific and highest-quality vibecoders are ranked
- **AI Matching** — where our AI agent recommends you to clients based on their project needs

The more you build and ship, the higher you climb.
