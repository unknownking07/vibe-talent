# Getting Hired

VibeTalent is designed to make you discoverable to clients who need reliable developers. Here's how the hiring flow works and how to maximize your chances.

## How Clients Find You

Clients discover vibecoders through three channels:

### 1. Explore Page
The main marketplace at `/explore`. Clients can:
- **Search** by username, bio, or tech stack
- **Filter** by badge level
- **Sort** by Vibe Score (default), streak length, or project count

Your profile card shows your avatar, bio, streak, badge, Vibe Score, and top tech stack tags. Make them count.

### 2. Leaderboard
The `/leaderboard` page ranks all vibecoders. The top 3 get prominent "podium" placement. Being on the first page of the leaderboard is prime real estate.

### 3. AI Matching
Clients can use the AI agent to describe their project, and the agent recommends the best-fit vibecoders based on:
- **Skill overlap** — Does your tech stack match their needs?
- **Consistency** — Active streak and badge level
- **Project quality** — Verified projects with live demos
- **Reviews** — Past client feedback
- **Activity recency** — Have you been active recently?

## What Makes You Stand Out

| Signal | What It Tells Clients |
|---|---|
| **High Vibe Score** | You're consistent AND you ship quality work |
| **Active streak** | You're currently coding — not dormant |
| **Badge level** | You've sustained effort over months |
| **Verified projects** | You actually built what you claim |
| **Live demos** | Clients can see your work right now |
| **5-star reviews** | Other clients trust you |
| **Complete profile** | You're professional and serious |

## How Hire Requests Work

### Step 1: Client Sends a Request
When a client clicks **"Hire This Vibecoder"** on your profile, they fill out a short form:
- Their name and email
- A message describing what they need
- Their budget

They don't need an account — it's designed to be frictionless.

### Step 2: You Get Notified
You'll receive:
- An **email notification** with the client's details
- An **in-app notification** in your Dashboard

### Step 3: Review and Respond
In your Dashboard, you'll see all hire requests. For each one you can:
- **Read** the full message and budget
- **Reply** — send your response directly
- **Delete** — remove requests you're not interested in

### Step 4: Chat
After the initial exchange, you and the client can continue the conversation through VibeTalent's built-in chat. Both sides can send messages without needing accounts.

### Step 5: Get Reviewed
After working together, clients can leave a **review** on your profile:
- 1-5 star rating
- Written comment
- Connected to the specific hire request

Reviews directly boost your Vibe Score (up to +50 points) and show future clients that you deliver.

## Tips for Getting More Hire Requests

1. **Ship real projects** — Verified projects with live URLs are your best marketing
2. **Fill out your social links** — Telegram and Twitter make you easy to contact
3. **Keep your streak alive** — Active streaks signal availability
4. **Write a compelling bio** — "Full-stack vibecoder specializing in AI + SaaS" beats "I code stuff"
5. **Respond quickly** — Clients often reach out to multiple vibecoders. First to respond wins.
6. **Ask for reviews** — After a successful project, ask your client to leave a review on VibeTalent
