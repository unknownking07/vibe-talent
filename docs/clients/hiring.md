# Hiring a Builder

Hiring on VibeTalent is designed to be fast and frictionless. You don't need an account — just find a builder you like and reach out.

## Sending a Hire Request

1. **Visit the builder's profile** — Find them via Explore, Leaderboard, or AI matching
2. **Click "Hire This Vibecoder"**
3. **Fill out the form:**

| Field | What to Include |
|---|---|
| **Your Name** | So the builder knows who you are |
| **Your Email** | For the builder to respond (no spam, promise) |
| **Message** | Describe your project, timeline, and what you're looking for |
| **Budget** | Give a range — it helps the builder decide quickly |

4. **Submit** — That's it!

### Tips for a Great Hire Request

- **Be specific** about what you need built ("React dashboard with Stripe integration" > "I need a website")
- **Mention your timeline** ("Need this in 2 weeks" helps the builder assess fit)
- **Share your budget range** — Builders are more likely to respond when expectations are clear
- **Explain why you chose them** — "I saw your streak and your SaaS projects" shows you did your homework

## What Happens Next

### Builder Gets Notified
Your request arrives in the builder's inbox immediately. They also get an email notification.

### Builder Responds
The builder will review your request and either:
- **Reply** with their thoughts, questions, or availability
- **Pass** if it's not a good fit (no hard feelings!)

### Continue the Conversation
After the initial exchange, you can chat back and forth through VibeTalent's built-in messaging. No account needed — your hire request ID acts as your access key.

## After the Project

### Leave a Review

Once you've worked with a builder, please leave a review! Your feedback:
- Helps other clients make better decisions
- Directly boosts the builder's Vibe Score
- Makes the whole marketplace more trustworthy

**To leave a review:**
1. Go to the builder's profile
2. Scroll to the Reviews section
3. Rate 1-5 stars and write a comment
4. Submit

You can leave up to 3 reviews per day.

## No Account Required

This is intentional. We believe hiring should be as easy as possible. You can:
- Browse all builder profiles
- View the leaderboard
- Send hire requests
- Chat with builders
- Leave reviews

...all without creating an account. The only people who need accounts are builders themselves.

## Safety & Privacy

- **Your email is private** — only visible to the builder you contact
- **Disposable emails are blocked** — to prevent spam
- **Rate limits** protect builders from being flooded with requests
- **The report system** lets you flag any problematic projects or profiles
