# Streaks & Badges

Streaks and badges are the core of your VibeTalent reputation. Streaks show you're active *right now*. Badges prove you've been consistent *over time*.

## How Streaks Work

A streak counts the number of **consecutive days** you've logged coding activity.

1. **Log activity** — Click "Log Activity" on your Dashboard, or let [GitHub sync](github-sync.md) do it for you
2. **One log per day** — Multiple clicks on the same day still count as 1 day
3. **Don't miss a day** — If you skip a day, your streak resets to 0
4. **Timezone** — Streaks are based on UTC. The day resets at midnight UTC.

### Example

```
Mon: Logged ✓  → Streak: 1
Tue: Logged ✓  → Streak: 2
Wed: Logged ✓  → Streak: 3
Thu: Missed ✗  → Streak: 0  (reset!)
Fri: Logged ✓  → Streak: 1  (start over)
```

### Tips for Maintaining Your Streak

- **Connect GitHub** — If you're already pushing code daily, your streak logs automatically
- **Set a reminder** — Log your activity before midnight UTC
- **Streak warnings** — VibeTalent sends you a notification at 6 PM UTC if you haven't logged that day
- **It's okay to restart** — Everyone breaks a streak eventually. Your longest streak is saved forever.

## Badge System

Badges are **permanent achievements** earned through sustained streaks. Once you earn a badge, it's yours forever — even if your streak resets.

### Badge Tiers

| Badge | Requirement | Vibe Score Bonus | What It Proves |
|---|---|---|---|
| **None** | Haven't hit 30 days yet | +0 | Just getting started |
| **Bronze** | 30+ day streak | +10 | You can show up consistently for a month |
| **Silver** | 90+ day streak | +20 | Three months of daily coding — serious commitment |
| **Gold** | 180+ day streak | +30 | Half a year. You're a machine. |
| **Diamond** | 365+ day streak | +40 | A full year of daily coding. Elite status. |

### How Badges Work

- Badges are based on your **longest streak**, not your current streak
- They **never get revoked** — Gold is Gold forever
- They're determined by the **highest qualifying tier**
- They appear on your profile, explore cards, and leaderboard

### Badge Progression Example

```
Day 1-29:   No badge yet, keep going!
Day 30:     🥉 Bronze earned! +10 Vibe Score bonus
Day 31-89:  Still Bronze, streak growing...
Day 90:     🥈 Silver earned! +20 bonus (replaces +10)
Day 91-179: Silver badge, getting close to Gold...
Day 180:    🥇 Gold earned! +30 bonus
Day 200:    Streak breaks (missed a day)
            → Current streak resets to 0
            → Badge stays Gold (based on longest_streak = 200)
            → Vibe Score drops from streak loss, but Gold bonus stays
```

### Where Badges Appear

- Next to your username on your **profile page**
- On your **explore card** when clients browse builders
- On the **leaderboard** next to your rank
- On your **share card** for social media

Badges are a powerful trust signal. When a client sees a Gold or Diamond badge, they know you've proven yourself over months of consistent work.
